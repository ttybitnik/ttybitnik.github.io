Rendez-vous 1408

Sound waves that reverberated between 2014 and 2018 in my old apartment.

I have fond memories of these songs. They used to play during the soirée nights, the casual gatherings with dear friends, and the late-night pool dips during my college days at 1408. The melodic tunes and lyrics that once filled the air of that apartment now refresh my memory of those times.
