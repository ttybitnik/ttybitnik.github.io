Party of the bothered ones

A bothered person talks about their bother. Some bothered people agree, other bothered people disagree. At the party, the unbothered people are divided into two groups of bothered people: those who are not bothered by the annoyance itself, but by the fact that there are so many bothered people bothering; and those who are not bothered by anything at all, except for the unbothered being bothered by so many bothered people, which is known to cause them deep annoyance.

In the end, the only ones truly not bothered were those who decided not to go to the party—which, certainly, bothered a lot of people. That is, it seemed that way… at least until they realized they had not even been invited.
