I write to discover what I think

The other day, I was reminded of my high school math teacher and how combinatorics was one of the few topics that interested me. The ability to envision an universe of possibilities within a given scenario was delightful. This morning, I combined that memory with an idea that has been occupying my mind lately—writing as an exercise of reflection and discovery. The lessons never end…

Combinatorial analysis exercise I

I write to discover what I think.
I think to write what I discover.
I discover to think what I write.
I write to think what I discover.
I think to discover what I write.
I discover to write what I think.
