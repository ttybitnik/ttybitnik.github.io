Hey there!

I'm Vinícius Moraes and welcome to my corner on the internet. This is where I share my passions, creations and thoughts on various topics, including technology, cinema, philosophy, literature, and language. You can check out the sitemap and the latest subjects below.

The content is available in both English and Portuguese. Usually I start writing in my native language, and sometimes, I begin in the opposite direction. However, regardless of the order, translation ideas always come up as I write. In the end, which was what is lost during the process.
