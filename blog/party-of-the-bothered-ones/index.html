Party of the bothered ones

A person who is bothered talks about something that is annoying them. Some people who are bothered agree with them, while others who are bothered disagree. At the party, those who are not bothered are divided into two groups: those who are not bothered by the annoyance itself, but by the fact that there are so many bothered people bothering them; and those who are not bothered by anything at all, except for those who are not bothered by being bothered by so many bothers, which is known to cause them deep annoyance.

In the end, the only people who decided not to go to the party were those who were not bothered at all, which certainly bothered a lot of people. Or at least that is how it seemed until they realized they were not even invited.
